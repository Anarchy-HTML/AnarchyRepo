The Crimson Creeper Lounge (CCL)

What we stand for:

In March of 2024, some friends made a server titled
“Rolism Royale”, though it would not get that name until mid July. The
community was OK, and most of us settled for a while.
However, around June, a lot of members, particularly staff or ex-staff, started
to realise how awful the community really was. When our owner,
Creepy, was banned, that was the final straw for many of us, and about
a month later, Mir and Nameless left the server, creating
their own server titled “Crimson Cross”. A little while later, we merged
with Creepy’s own server, creating CCL. We strive to be the
server Rolism Royale couldn’t be, and have a welcoming,
active community with little-to-no flaws.
